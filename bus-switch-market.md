<p><ul><li>Touch Type</li><li>Press Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bus Switch market is categorized into two primary types: touch type and press type. Touch type bus switches operate through capacitive sensing, allowing users to activate them with a light touch, enabling sleek designs and reducing mechanical wear. In contrast, press type bus switches require physical pressing to operate, providing tactile feedback and reliable performance. Both types are essential in various applications, from consumer electronics to automotive controls, catering to different user preferences and design requirements.</p></p>
