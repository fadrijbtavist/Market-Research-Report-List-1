<p><p>The endovascular stent graft market is categorized into three main types: synthetic, biological, and biosynthetic. Synthetic stent grafts are made from durable materials like polyester or ePTFE, offering strength and resistance to degradation. Biological stent grafts utilize natural tissues, promoting better integration with the body but may have limited durability. Biosynthetic stent grafts combine synthetic and biological materials, aiming to enhance biocompatibility while maintaining structural integrity, offering a balance between durability and biological performance in vascular interventions.</p></p>
