<p><p>Beam couplings are flexible mechanical components used to connect shafts while accommodating misalignment. The market is primarily divided into two types: single beam couplings and multi-beam couplings. Single beam couplings feature one flexible beam, suitable for applications with minimal misalignment, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, multi-beam couplings consist of several beams, allowing greater flexibility and better performance in applications with higher misalignment and torque requirements. This diversification enhances adaptability in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
