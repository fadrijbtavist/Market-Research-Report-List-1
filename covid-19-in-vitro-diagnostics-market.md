<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The COVID-19 in-vitro diagnostics market comprises various testing methodologies. Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDTs) provide quick results using lateral flow technology, suitable for on-site use. Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA) are laboratory-based tests that detect antibodies or antigens, offering high sensitivity and specificity for COVID-19 diagnosis. Neutralization Assays measure the ability of antibodies to inhibit viral infection, providing insights into immune responses. Together, these methods support effective COVID-19 detection and monitoring in diverse healthcare settings.</p></p>
