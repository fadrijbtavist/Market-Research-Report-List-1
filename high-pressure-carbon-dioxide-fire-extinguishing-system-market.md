<p><p>The High Pressure Carbon Dioxide Fire Extinguishing System market features two primary types: Unit Independent Fire Extinguishing Systems and Combined Distribution Fire Extinguishing Systems. Unit Independent Systems operate autonomously in designated areas, providing targeted fire suppression without reliance on a larger network. In contrast, Combined Distribution Systems consist of a network of pipes and nozzles that distribute carbon dioxide across multiple zones, enabling comprehensive coverage and protection for larger facilities. Both systems effectively mitigate fire risks in various industrial applications.</p></p>
