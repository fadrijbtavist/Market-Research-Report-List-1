<p><p>The Specialty Inorganic Catalysts market is divided into two main types: heterogeneous and homogeneous catalysts. Heterogeneous catalysts are solids that facilitate reactions in a different phase, often used in processes like chemical synthesis and petroleum refining. In contrast, homogeneous catalysts are in the same phase as the reactants, typically dissolving in a solvent, which allows for better molecular interaction, improving reaction rates. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing efficiency and selectivity in various industrial applications.</p></p>
