<p><p>Stereotactic-guided biopsy instruments are advanced medical devices used to accurately obtain tissue samples from specific areas within the body, particularly in soft tissues such as the breast or brain. Technological innovations, such as the development of robotic-assisted biopsy systems and improved imaging modalities, are also enhancing the efficacy and safety of these procedures.
