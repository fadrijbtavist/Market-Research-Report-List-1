<p><p>The hi-fi earphone market is categorized into three main types: in-ear, on-ear, and over-ear. On-ear earphones rest on the outer ear, balancing comfort and sound quality, often favored for casual listening. Over-ear earphones completely envelop the ears, delivering superior audio fidelity and comfort for extended use, making them popular among audiophiles and gamers seeking immersive experiences.</p></p>
