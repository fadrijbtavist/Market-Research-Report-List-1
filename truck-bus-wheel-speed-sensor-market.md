<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Truck & Bus Wheel Speed Sensor market includes two primary types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to detect wheel speed changes, providing precise and reliable data for vehicle stability and anti-lock braking systems. In contrast, Magnetic Electric sensors generate voltage based on the magnetic field's variations caused by wheel rotation, offering a simpler and cost-effective solution. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety and performance in commercial transportation applications.</p></p>
