<p><ul><li>Bevel Gear Reducers</li><li>Worm Gear Reducer</li><li>Helical Gear Reducers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The right-angle gear reducer market comprises three main types: bevel gear reducers, worm gear reducers, and helical gear reducers. Bevel gear reducers use conical gears to allow high-speed torque transmission at right angles, making them suitable for compact applications. Worm gear reducers offer high torque reduction and self-locking capabilities, ideal for vertical setups. Helical gear reducers feature angled teeth for smoother operation and higher load capacity, commonly used in industrial machinery for efficient power transmission at various orientations.</p></p>
