<p><p>Synthetic leather for furniture encompasses various materials, including PVC, Normal PU, Microfiber PU, and Ecological function PU. PVC offers durability and water resistance but lacks breathability. Normal PU, known for its softness and versatility, is commonly used for upholstery. Microfiber PU mimics the look and feel of real leather while being lightweight and easy to clean. Ecological function PU focuses on sustainability, using eco-friendly processes and materials, making it a popular choice for environmentally conscious consumers.</p></p>
